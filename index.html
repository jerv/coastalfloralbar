How It Works

  1. 1

    Choose Your Colors

    Pick up to 4 colors to include in your package and note any colors you do not want included.

  2. 2

    Budget vs. Premium Focal Flowers

    Budget: Chrysanthemum, Alstroemeria, Carnation, Rose, etc.

    Premium: Lily, Peony, Dahlia, Ranunculus, Garden Rose, etc.

  3. 3

    Finalize Details

    Confirm guest/bouquet count and choose full service or self-serve.

  4. 4

    Sign & We Deliver

    Sign your contract and we bring everything to your event.
